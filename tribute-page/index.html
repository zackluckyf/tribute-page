Bill Gates

A revolutionary business magnate and titan of industry

Here's a chronological list of his accomplishments!

  • 1968 - Begins programming computers at age 13.
  • 1973 - Bill Gates enrolls at Harvard University
  • 1975 - Bill Gates and Paul Allen form partnership called Micro-Soft
  • 1980 - Microsoft partners with IBM to wrtie the BASIC for the IBM PC
  • 1981 - Microsoft becomes incorporated and Bill Gates becomes President
  • 1985 - Gates introduces the Microsoft Windows operating system to compete with Apple.
  • 1987 - Becomes the youngest U.S. billionaire, worth $1.25 billion at 31 years old.
  • 1989 - Bill Gates founds corbis
  • 1995 - Windows 95 launches and sells more than one million copies in four days.
  • 1995 - Bill Gates becomes the richest man in the world
  • 1998 - United States v. Microsoft Trial
  • 2001 - Microsoft reaches a settlement with the Justice Department in the antitrust case.
  • 2005 - Named Time Magazine's "Person of the Year" along with his wife Melinda and musician Bono.
  • 2008 - Gates retires from day-to-day work at Microsoft to concentrate on philanthropy.
  • 2010 - Gates Foundation announces it will donate $10 billion for vaccine research.
  • 2015 - Ranked No. 1 on Forbes' list of the world's billionaires with a net worth of $79 billion.
